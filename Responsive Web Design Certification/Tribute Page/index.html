Randi Berry:

Indie Theater Superhero.

Timeline of Randi Berry's life.

  • 1977: Born in Brooklyn, NY.
  • 1999: Graduated from the New World School of the Arts with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theater.
  • 2000: Moves to New York City and co-founds the Wreckio Ensemble Theater Company.
  • 2011: Elected as a Board Member for the League of Independent Theater.
  • 2012: Co-founds the Indie Theater Fund (née the LIT Fund).
  • 2016: Co-founds Indie Space.

"...it’s not until we get over the hump of thinking it’s not possible that anything can happen."
